Seven College of Communication and Information Women Named to Mothers, Mentors and Muses Class

It’s the end to another year, and, as we’re committed to staying connected with our advertising alumni, we’d like to tell you a bit about our successful year. Franklin Advertising, the student-run advertising organization at Kent State, had a busy year, and we’re looking forward to building on it next year. 

Latest Student Updates

At the beginning of the spring semester, we began work for two clients: one, a small business and the second, a campus initiative. Arike’s Kitchen is a small catering business in Columbus, Ohio. Arike’s Kitchen started a subscription box service that includes various African foods and items with the goal of introducing the country to the “real” Africa while providing school lunches to children in African public education. Our work involved naming the subscription box, designing the actual box and creating a crowdfunding campaign from strategy to tactical plan development.
